ABOUT MYSELF
============

I am currently a junior at UNC Chapel Hill double majoring in Biostatistics, B.S.P.H. and Mathematics, B.S. with a minor in Chemistry. 

I have been working at [UNC BRAIN Lab](https://bbm.web.unc.edu/) in the [Biomedical Research and Imaging Center (BRIC)](https://www.med.unc.edu/bric/) since September 2021. Currently, I am also working as a Lab Assistant for BIOL 252L Human Anatomy & Physiology. I have been volunteering at the Emergency Department at UNC Hospitals since last January.
